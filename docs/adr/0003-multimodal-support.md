# 0003-multimodal-support.md

We decided to implement Multi-Modal Support by adding analysis for Image Descriptions (Alt-text). This choice aligns with the context of a Social Writing App (Medium/Substack) and provides transparency for accessibility-related content.

## Context
Standard text analysis (Stylometrics, Burstiness) is optimized for long-form content. Image Descriptions are typically short and follow different patterns when generated by AI versus written by humans.

## Decision
1. **Content Type**: Support "Image Descriptions" (Alt-text) in addition to standard text.
2. **Detection Signals**:
   - **Template Conformity**: Detects formulaic patterns typical of AI (e.g., "A photo of...", "An image showing...").
   - **Descriptive Verbosity**: Measures the clinical and overly detailed nature of AI descriptions compared to human subjectivity.
3. **Architecture**:
   - Create `multimodal_scoring.py` to encapsulate Alt-text signals.
   - Update the Scoring Engine to route analysis based on an explicit `content_type` field in the `/submit` request.
4. **Database**: Update `audit_log` to store `content_type` and new signal scores for traceability.

## Consequences
- The `/submit` endpoint now requires a `content_type` field (defaulting to "text" for backward compatibility).
- New specific weights are applied for Alt-text to prioritize Template Conformity as a strong AI marker.
